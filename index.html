What I'm passionate about

I'm really passionate about getting women and girls interested and involved in Computer Science. I am a product of women-in-CS initiatives like Girls Who Code and an all-girl robotics team. I believe that in order for any team to achieve their best results, they need diverse ideas and solutions, which means they need a diverse team!

I'm also passionate about sustainability! As humans, we are stewards to the Earth and are called to take care of it! I try to do my part by replacing single-use items in my daily life, buying products with minimal to no packaging, and eating vegetarian/vegan when I can, as well as by supporting companies with sustainable practices.

I also think it's really important that young people learn financial literacy, from managing a bank account and budgeting to how the stock market works and how to manage debt. Living on your own and learning how to "adult" is hard, but knowing how to manage your finances makes it a little easier.
